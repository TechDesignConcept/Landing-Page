The approach

Codify, gate, pave.

Estates managed by portal click accumulate drift, tribal knowledge, and audit findings. The remedy is engineering discipline applied in order: codify the estate (Bicep first, Azure Verified Modules where they fit), gate the pipeline (what-if analysis, policy checks, reviews that mean something), and pave golden paths so workload teams get governed infrastructure without needing to become platform experts.

The handover philosophy applies here more than anywhere: pipelines and modules your engineers own and extend, documented well enough that the consultancy is not the operating manual.
