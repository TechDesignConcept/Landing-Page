Why these numbers

Credible, not cheap.

The Australian market for senior Azure consulting sits at A$1,800–A$3,000 per day for independents. Big 4 and tier-1 boutiques charge A$2,800–A$4,500 commercially but stack project managers and junior architects on top.

Tech Design Concept is a Principal-only boutique with regulated-industry experience. The Principal rate sits alongside tier-1 panel pricing but delivers a named senior practitioner - no pyramid, no hand-offs. The Senior engineer rate exists for defined execution work where a Principal isn't warranted.

If your engagement fits a productised offer, take it. You'll get more value, a clearer scope, and a fixed price. The day rates are the escape hatch - not the default.
